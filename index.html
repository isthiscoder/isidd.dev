Vision

I want to build AI systems that are fast, honest, and genuinely useful — not just impressive demos.

The most important work in AI right now isn't in the size of the model — it's in the quality of the interface between intelligence and action. The next decade belongs to engineers who can close that gap.

My focus: inference efficiency, reliable agentic systems, and evaluation methodology — the unglamorous plumbing that determines whether AI actually ships.

If you're building something at the frontier — at OpenAI, Anthropic, NVIDIA, Google DeepMind, or a small team that thinks it can change things — I want to be in the room where it happens.
